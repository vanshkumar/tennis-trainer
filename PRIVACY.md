# Privacy Policy for Tennis Trainer

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Tennis Trainer is an iPhone app for tennis practice feedback. It uses on-device camera and video analysis to help detect tennis movement cues, such as the apex of a serve toss.

## Information We Collect

Tennis Trainer does not sell user data, use advertising tracking, or share personal data with third parties.

The app may use:

- Camera access, so you can analyze live tennis practice.
- Selected video access, so you can analyze a video you choose from your device.
- Audio playback, so the app can provide feedback sounds during practice.

Video frames, pose detection, ball detection, and training feedback are processed on device. Tennis Trainer does not upload your camera feed, selected videos, or analysis results to a server.

## How Information Is Used

Camera and selected video data are used only to provide the app's tennis training features, including visual overlays and audio cues.

If any video, image, or analysis data is stored by the app, it is stored locally on your device unless you choose to share it yourself.

## Third Parties

Tennis Trainer does not use third-party advertising networks or tracking services.

If you provide feedback through TestFlight or contact us directly, Apple or your email provider may process that communication according to their own privacy policies.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Tennis Trainer does not maintain a remote user account or remote user database.

You can delete locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device. You can also delete any videos or media from your device's Photos library using Apple's Photos app.

## Children's Privacy

Tennis Trainer is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through beta feedback or support contact, please contact us so we can address it.
